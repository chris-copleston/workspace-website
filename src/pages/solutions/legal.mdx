## The Problem

Top global law firms, especially the top 25, face unique challenges in adapting to the evolving demands of hybrid work, while maintaining the high standards of client service and office operations expected by their teams and clients. Traditional, old-school practices persist, creating friction in areas like employee flexibility, office attendance management, and meeting room experiences.

## Key challenges include:

- **Hybrid Work Dilemmas**: Balancing the need for in-office presence with the desire for flexible, hybrid working conditions.
- **Employee Attendance Monitoring**: Regulating and monitoring office attendance, ensuring proper space utilization while maintaining a premium office experience.
- **Client Experience Expectations**: High-end, VIP services are expected from top legal firms, requiring seamless, personalized client interactions and professional support during meetings.
- **Outdated Practices**: Overcoming traditional working methods that hinder efficiency, flexibility, and modern client expectations.

## How New Wave Workspace Solves These Problems

New Wave Workspace provides a tailored solution to help law firms modernize operations while maintaining the professional prestige expected of top-tier legal services.

## Our capabilities include:

- **Flexible Quota Workplace Policies**: Ensure optimal in-office attendance by combining hybrid work with smart analytics, allowing law firms to regulate employee presence based on data-driven insights.
- **Visitor Management System**: Deliver a "Tapis Rouge" experience by managing client visits seamlessly, ensuring personalized service that creates lasting impressions and enhances client satisfaction.
