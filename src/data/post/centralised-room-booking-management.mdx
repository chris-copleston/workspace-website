# Designed for Front-of-House, Built for Exceptional Client Experience

## Overview

New Wave delivers a centralised, reception-controlled room booking platform purpose-built for organisations where Front of House and Facilities teams play a critical role in the meeting experience.

Unlike Outlook-based room bookings — designed for individual calendar convenience — New Wave empowers receptionists and facilities concierge teams to actively manage rooms, services, and guest experience in real time.

## Key Unique Selling Points (USPs)

### Centralised Reception Control

- Single operational view of all meeting rooms
- Receptionists can create, edit, move or cancel bookings on behalf of guests
- No dependency on individual user calendars

### Drag-and-Drop Room Management

<Image src={BookingManagementImage.src} alt="Drag-and-Drop Room Management" />

- Intuitive visual interface for instant room reallocation
- Resolve last-minute changes without friction
- Optimise room utilisation in real time

### Service-Led Bookings (Beyond the Room)

<Image src={BookingAddCateringImage.src} alt="Service-Led Bookings (Beyond the Room)" />

- Attach catering, IT equipment, and dedicated support teams directly to bookings
- Services follow the booking — even when rooms change
- Eliminates manual follow-ups and service gaps

### Reception-Controlled Email Communication

- Fully customisable email templates
- Reception decides what, when, and to whom communications are sent
- Consistent, professional guest experience — independent of Outlook rules

### Facilities Concierge Tablet Experience

<Image src={CentralisedRoomBookingManagementImage.src} alt="Facilities Concierge Tablet Experience" />

Purpose-built for Front of House desks and concierge teams:

- Tablet-based interface for fast operation
- Guest check-in / check-out
- Automatic no-show detection
- Live room status updates
- Immediate visibility of upcoming arrivals and services

Designed for speed, clarity, and hospitality-grade service.
